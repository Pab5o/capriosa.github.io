Medical Onboarding Germany

Medical Onboarding Germany wurde 2012 von uns für die MCM Media Consult Maier und Partner GmbH entwickelt.

In October 2012 MedicalOnboardingGermany »MOG« has been found as an international social recruiting project, powered by MCM Media Consult Maier und Partner GmbH, Karlsruhe, Germany. Our international recruiting facebook fanpage got more than 3.000 international medical-candidate-fans within only few month - the ideal platform to present your job offers there! Our recently launched website provides international medical professionals with important information about recognition procedures, learning German for doctors, career chances, living in Germany and more. International medical professionals can apply on this website or directly on our »MOG Check« on Facebook. »MOG« offers employers in the healthcare sector the opportunity to present vacancies as well or to participate in our recruiting events.
